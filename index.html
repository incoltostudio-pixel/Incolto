INCOLTO

Incolto Studio

Founded in 2019, Incolto creates site-specific projects blending social design, participatory practices and visual arts. Centred on regeneration as a shared practice of care, their work fosters new ways of inhabiting places through simple, relational interventions that spark urban rituals and intergenerational ties.

We collaborate with public institutions, urban planning and architecture firms, placemakers, cultural centers, and festivals.

- Social design
- Workshops
- Public space interventions
- Temporary installation
- Visual design and storytelling
